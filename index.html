This project is designed to let you and your friends place small bets on drinks or other fun challenges, creating unforgettable evenings together. It’s simple, free, and easy to use: pick a game you like, and the loser drinks—or make up your own rules.

The project started as a spontaneous hobby. I had always been looking for something like this, and alongside my work, I decided to bring this little idea to life. I hope you enjoy it, and I’d be thrilled if you shared it so that even more people can join in—from Malaysia to Mexico, from New Zealand to Sweden. Bet all over the world!
